Model version I (left) vs. Model version II (right)

Presenting annual means (from monthly climatological means) from simulations with the following casenames:

ModI = 53OSLO_PD_UNTUNED and 53OSLO_PI_wPDoxi
ModII = 53OsloTstNuc and PI53OsloTstNuc

Both ModI and ModII are from the featureCAM5-OsloDevelopment_trunk2.0-6 set up with
MG1.5, CLM4.5, online DMS/POM, with Moz gas deposition scheme, and both using PD oxidants for PI.

Only difference in PI53OsloTstNuc: self coagulation of MODE_IDX_SO4SOA_AIT is allowed to
contribute to the cogulation sink in the equation for the nucleation rate (coagS), but with
that coagulate instead ending up on the already existing coagulation receivers (Mode 5-10).

ModI & II (year 2006-2010) is from ERA nudged simulations (UVPS), using compset NUDGEPTAEROUPD1.

ModI PD: CAM5.3-Oslo, 2000 emissions (PD oxidants), 2 years spin-up, nudged to ERA (2006-2010)

ModI PI: CAM5.3-Oslo, 1850 emissions (PD oxidants), 2 years spin-up, nudged to ERA (2006-2010)

ModII PD: CAM5.3-Oslo, 2000 emissions (PD oxidants), 2 years spin-up, nudged to ERA (2006-2010)

ModII PI: CAM5.3-Oslo, 1850 emissions (PD oxidants), 2 years spin-up, nudged to ERA (2006-2010)
